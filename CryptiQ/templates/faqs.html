Frequently Asked Questions

Search the FAQ or expand any question. If something’s missing, post it as feedback or DM me via a post.

—
What are Labs?⌄

Labs are private workspaces where you can store your notes/plaintext, ciphertext, images, and tool outputs. They autosave as you work.

What does Labs Pro unlock?⌄

Unlimited Labs, unlimited image tabs, plus premium Lab features like PDF export, cloning, and history snapshots.

How does Lab History work?⌄

On Pro, snapshots are created on saves. You can restore any snapshot from the History modal. Restoring overwrites your current lab content.

What’s included in PDF export?⌄

Your lab title, notes/plaintext, ciphertext, and uploaded images. It’s designed to be printable and shareable.

What’s the difference between Tools and the Breaker?⌄

Tools are focused transforms/analysis (like frequency, spacing, replace). The Breaker tries to automatically solve a cipher (or narrow it down) for supported cipher types.

How does the Substitution grid work?⌄

It’s a live mapping: you enter ciphertext letters A–Z under each plaintext letter marker. The cipher box updates in real-time so you can see patterns quickly.

Does Pro affect Weekly Cipher or leaderboards?⌄

No — Weekly Cipher scoring stays fair. Pro is about Labs capacity + QoL features.

How do notifications work?⌄

The bell shows unread notifications. Opening the dropdown fetches them and marks them as read. Clicking a notification takes you to the linked page.

Are Labs private?⌄

Yes — Labs are private to your account. Public content is only what you post in Posts / Weekly Cipher.

Do I need an account?⌄

You can browse as a guest, but you need an account to create Labs, post, comment, or enter Weekly Cipher.

I found a bug — how do I report it?⌄

Make a post describing what happened (screenshots help). Include the page and steps to reproduce.
