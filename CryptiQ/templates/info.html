Caesar Cipher

The Caesar cipher shifts every letter by the same number of positions in the alphabet. It‚Äôs one of the simplest ciphers in history and gets its name from Julius Caesar, who reportedly used it in wartime messages.

Write your message in rows beneath a keyword, then read off the columns in the order of the letters in that keyword.
