About the Weekly Cipher

The Weekly Cipher is The Cipher Lab’s rotating cryptographic challenge. Each week, a new puzzle is released — ranging from simple substitution ciphers to advanced transpositions and polyalphabetic encryption.

  • 🔐 Weekly Challenges
    Every Monday, a new cipher is posted. You can solve it and submit your plaintext or keyword. Correct answers earn points — faster solves earn more.
  • 🏆 Seasons
    The year is divided into multiple seasons (each lasting a few months). Seasonal leaderboards reset at the start of each season, giving everyone a fresh chance to climb.
  • ⚡ Fastest Weekly Solvers
    Each week, the quickest correct submissions are featured on the Weekly Fastest board — rewarding both speed and accuracy. Only your fastest solve per week counts.
  • 📊 All-Time Rankings
    Your total score across all seasons is tracked in the All-Time Leaderboard, showcasing the most consistent and dedicated solvers.
  • 💡 Hints & Scoring
    Some puzzles have hints. You can reveal them anytime — but each hint slightly reduces the points you can earn for that week’s cipher.

🧠 Tip: Each challenge may use different classical ciphers — Caesar, Vigenère, Affine, Railfence, AMSCO, or others. Pay attention to patterns, letter frequencies, and clues in the text.

New puzzles appear every Monday at 12:00 UTC — good luck, and happy decrypting!
